Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NightTales handles information when you use this application.

1. Information We Collect

This application does not collect personal information from end users. OAuth tokens used to authenticate with social media platforms are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any third party other than the respective platform APIs.

2. How We Use Information

Authentication tokens are used solely to publish video content to connected social media accounts. No personal data is shared beyond what is necessary to operate the platform integrations.

3. Data Storage

All data (tokens, video files, content logs) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate external servers that store your personal data.

4. Third-Party Platforms

When you connect a TikTok account, TikTok's own privacy policy governs how your data is handled.

5. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
